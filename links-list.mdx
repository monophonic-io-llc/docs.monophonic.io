The "Links List" is your new "link in bio". Visit your dashboard and click on "Links List" in the main navigation under the "Site" heading.

Here, you'll be able to add links of any kind you like.

When you're finished, visit [your-site.com]/links (or [your-artist-name].monophonic.site)/links and you'll have a dedicated page of links you can use as a "link in bio".

Here's an example: https://melindaandthenightsky.com/links
