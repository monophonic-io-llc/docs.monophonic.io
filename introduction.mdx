# Wait a minute, what is Monophonic.io?

Monophonic.io is a **no-code website builder designed specifically for musicians, bands, stand-up comics, and artists**.

Our goal is to make it dead simple to make a website, and price it affordably, so artists can spend their time and money on their craft.

## How It Works

1. **Sign up** for a free 14-day trial at [manage.monophonic.site](https://manage.monophonic.site)
2. **Customize your design** with colors, fonts, and your logo
3. **Add your content** including tracks, videos, shows, photos, and merch
4. **Organize sections** to control how content appears on your site
5. **Connect your domain** or use your free monophonic.site subdomain

## What Makes Monophonic Different?

Unlike generic website builders like Squarespace or Wix, Monophonic is purpose-built for musicians with music-first design sections for tracks, videos, and shows.

It's significantly more affordable, faster to set up (minutes, not hours), and requires no coding or design experience.
